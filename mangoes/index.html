Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering Pakistani mangoes in Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area.

Where can I buy Pakistani mangoes in Toronto? expand_more

You can buy premium Pakistani mangoes — including Sindhri, Chaunsa, and Anwar Ratol — from Ameer Global. We offer free local pickup from our Mississauga Distribution Center and $6.99 flat-rate delivery across eligible GTA towns, including Toronto, Brampton, Scarborough, Markham, Vaughan, and Oakville. Simply visit this page and click "Purchase Order" on your preferred variety to get started.

What varieties of Pakistani mangoes are available? expand_more

Ameer Global offers three premium varieties, all hand-picked from our family farms in Multan, Pakistan:

  • Sindhri — The Honeyed Giant. Large, aromatic, and intensely sweet with a firm texture ideal for slicing.
  • Anwar Ratol — The Fragrant Gem. Small, ultra-sweet with an intoxicating floral nose. A connoisseur's favorite.
  • Chaunsa — The Crown Jewel. Creamy, buttery flesh with deep sweetness. Pakistan's most celebrated variety.
When is Pakistani mango season in Canada? expand_more

For the 2026 season, our premium Pakistani mangoes are expected to arrive in mid-July (tentatively July 10–15). Availability in Toronto and the GTA depends on air freight schedules. We recommend placing a pre-order or reserve order early to guarantee your supply.

Do you deliver Pakistani mangoes in Toronto and the GTA? expand_more

Yes! Ameer Global offers delivery to eligible GTA towns for a flat rate of $6.99. We deliver to eligible GTA towns including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Scarborough, Markham, Vaughan, and Oakville. We also offer free pickup from our Mississauga Distribution Center. All mangoes are transported using cold-chain logistics to ensure freshness.

How do I place an order for Pakistani mangoes? expand_more

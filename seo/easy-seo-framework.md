# Easy SEO Framework (Ameer Global Mangoes)

## Positioning Line (use everywhere)
`Pakistani mangoes in Toronto & the GTA — delivery across eligible GTA zones and free pickup from Mississauga.`

## Weekly Workflow (simple)
1. Publish 1 update on `/mangoes/` (arrival, stock, delivery window).
2. Publish 1 Google Business Profile post with Toronto + GTA terms.
3. Ask 3-5 customers for Google reviews mentioning city + mango variety.
4. Check Search Console for top queries and improve one page section.

## Page Formula (reuse for every city page)
1. Title: `Pakistani Mangoes in {City} | Ameer Global`
2. H1: `Pakistani Mangoes in {City}`
3. Paragraph: delivery + pickup promise.
4. CTA 1: delivery checkout link.
5. CTA 2: pickup checkout link.
6. Internal links: back to `/mangoes/` and other city pages.

## Content Rules
1. Mention `Toronto`, `GTA`, and `Pakistani mangoes` naturally.
2. Mention all three varieties: Sindhri, Chaunsa, Anwar Ratol.
3. Keep wording truthful: delivery in eligible GTA zones, pickup for all GTA customers.
4. Do not keyword-stuff; prioritize readable buyer-focused text.

## Technical Rules
1. Keep pages in sitemap.
2. Keep canonical URLs set.
3. Keep FAQ schema and LocalBusiness schema valid.
4. Request indexing for new/updated pages after deploy.

## Fast KPI Dashboard (track every week)
1. Impressions for `pakistani mangoes toronto`
2. Clicks to `/mangoes/` + city pages
3. Orders started from organic landing pages
4. Review count and average rating

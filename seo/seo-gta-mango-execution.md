# Ameer Global GTA Mango SEO Execution Plan

## 1) Core Targets
- Primary page: `https://ameerglobal.ca/mangoes/`
- Primary intent: transactional (`buy`, `delivery`, `pickup`, city + mango terms)
- Primary region: Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Oakville, Burlington

## 2) Keyword System
- Use [`keywords-gta-mangoes.csv`](/Users/hussainalihaq/Downloads/ameerglobal1/seo/keywords-gta-mangoes.csv).
- Focus order:
  1. `P0` keywords (money terms)
  2. `P1` local + variety terms
  3. `P2` informational support terms

## 3) On-Page Rules (must follow)
- Keep one main H1 containing `Pakistani Mangoes` + `Toronto/GTA`.
- Use city keywords naturally in body copy and FAQ (no stuffing).
- Add internal links:
  - from home page to `/mangoes/` with anchor text like `Pakistani Mangoes in GTA`
  - from product sections to checkout/reserve links
- Keep title under ~60 chars where possible and meta description under ~160 chars.
- Keep canonical set to `https://ameerglobal.ca/mangoes/`.

## 4) Technical SEO Checklist
- Ensure `sitemap.xml` is current and includes `/mangoes/`.
- Keep `robots.txt` with sitemap reference.
- Compress/optimize hero images and keep `loading`/`decoding` attributes.
- Remove blocking assets where possible and improve Core Web Vitals over time.

## 5) Local SEO (highest impact for GTA)
- Create/optimize Google Business Profile:
  - business name: Ameer Global INC.
  - category: fruit wholesaler / food supplier (best match)
  - service areas: GTA cities
  - phone, website, hours, delivery/pickup info
- Ask customers for Google reviews mentioning:
  - mango variety bought
  - city in GTA
  - delivery/pickup experience
- Post weekly GBP updates in season (arrivals, batches, sellout notices).

## 6) Authority Signals
- Get local mentions/backlinks from:
  - GTA South Asian food bloggers
  - local directories
  - community groups and events
- Keep NAP consistency (name/address/phone) identical across all profiles.

## 7) Tracking and Reporting
- Set up Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
- Track weekly:
  - clicks/impressions for `/mangoes/`
  - queries containing `mango`, `pakistani mangoes`, city names
  - conversion rate from `/mangoes/` to checkout
- Keep a simple KPI target:
  - +15-25% impressions month over month in season
  - improving average position for P0/P1 terms

## 8) Realistic Expectation
- SEO does not rank instantly. Typical timeframe:
  - 2-6 weeks for index/query movement
  - 2-4 months for stronger local ranking gains
- Biggest levers for your niche:
  - consistent local relevance
  - reviews mentioning city + mango variety
  - clean technical setup

The Varieties

Each variety is a distinct chapter in our heritage. Hand-selected, individually wrapped, and presented with the reverence they deserve.

The Honeyed Giant

Sindhri

Large, visually striking, and intensely aromatic. The Sindhri offers a perfect balance of tartness and honeyed sweetness, with a firm texture ideal for slicing.

The Crown Jewel

Anwar Ratol

Small in stature, immense in character. The Anwar Ratol is celebrated for its intense, floral aroma and remarkably sweet, fiberless flesh. A fleeting luxury of the mid-summer harvest.

The Nectar

Chaunsa

Renowned for its exceptionally rich, syrupy sweetness and minimal fiber. The Chaunsa is a late-season delicacy that offers a profound, melting texture.

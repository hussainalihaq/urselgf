Historical Record

History of Khewra Mines

The Khewra Salt Mines are among the oldest known mineral mines in South Asia and a cornerstone of Himalayan crystal extraction. Our sourcing partners preserve this heritage while maintaining modern quality and traceability standards.

Ancient Discovery

Oral and historical references connect Khewra's salt seams to early regional trade corridors, making it a long-standing strategic mineral source.

Colonial Era Structuring

Mining operations moved from artisan extraction toward structured corridors, improving consistency and opening formal export pathways.

Modern Khewra

Current operations combine selective extraction, laboratory checks, and refined logistics for edible, wellness, and décor-grade lines.
